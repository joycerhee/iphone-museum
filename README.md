# iPhone Evolution Museum

## Museum Concept
A digital museum exhibit tracing the evolution of Apple's iPhone from 2007 to 2025. 
The exhibit explores how changes in design, technology, and functionality shaped modern 
communication and culture. Intended for everyday technology users.

## Design Style — Minimalism
Clean white space, simple sans-serif typography, black and white palette, strict grid 
layout. Mirrors Apple's own visual language which reinforces the subject matter of the exhibit.

## Brand Archetype — The Creator
Visionary, innovative, world-changing tone throughout. The exhibit positions the iPhone 
as a creative and cultural force, not just a product. Every section frames innovation 
as something that changed how humans live.

## Cialdini Principle — Social Proof
Adoption statistics and cultural impact moments appear throughout the exhibit — 
"3 billion iPhones sold", "Instagram launched on iPhone", "Neural Engine reaches 
2 billion pockets" — to show visitors how broadly each generation was validated by 
real people.
